About Me (CV)

I am currently a third year master student in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering at Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Jian Yao. My research interests lie in the areas of image processing and computer vision. I have done some work on:
  • (1) image inpainting and completion
  • (2) affine image rectification
  • (3) image and video blending
  • (4) image and video stitching
    • In particular, I am now interested in some application problems (e.g., object detection and saliency detection) based on 360 panorama.
